Help & Support

Welcome to the Terminusa Crypto Tax Reporting System help page. Here you can find answers to common questions and contact information for further assistance.

Getting Started

  • Register an account using the registration page.
  • Login with your credentials.
  • Enter your Ethereum address on the dashboard to generate tax reports.
  • Manage your subscription and billing in the billing section.
  • Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) for enhanced security.

Contact Support

If you encounter any issues or have questions, please contact our support team at support@terminusa.com.

FAQs

  • How do I reset my password? Use the forgot password link on the login page.
  • What payment methods are accepted? Currently, we accept major credit cards.
  • How long does report generation take? Report generation typically takes a few minutes depending on transaction volume.
  • Is my data secure? Yes, we use industry-standard security practices to protect your data.
